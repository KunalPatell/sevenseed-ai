STAR Method Templates

Behavioral
Tell me about a time you solved a difficult problem
Situation: "In my [X] project / internship at [Company]…"
Task: "I was responsible for [specific task]…"
Action: "I [specific actions taken — use numbers]…"
Result: "This resulted in [quantified outcome]…"
Describe a time you worked under pressure
Situation: "During [deadline/project], we faced [constraint]…"
Task: "I needed to [deliver X] within [Y hours/days]…"
Action: "I prioritized by [method], delegated [task], and…"
Result: "We delivered on time and [positive outcome]…"
Tell me about a conflict with a teammate
Situation: "During [project], my teammate and I disagreed on [approach]…"
Task: "We needed to align quickly to keep the project on track…"
Action: "I scheduled a 1:1, listened to their perspective, and proposed…"
Result: "We reached consensus, shipped on time, and improved our process…"
What is your biggest achievement?
Situation: "The company/team was facing [challenge]…"
Task: "I was assigned to [lead/build/improve] X…"
Action: "I [specific technical actions + leadership]…"
Result: "Achieved [metric] — [% improvement / time saved / revenue]…"
Describe when you learned a new skill quickly
Situation: "Our project required [new tech] which I hadn't used before…"
Task: "I had [X days] to become productive in [skill]…"
Action: "I followed [resource], built [project], and applied it to…"
Result: "Within [time], I contributed [X] to the project successfully…"
Why do you want to work at this company?
Research angle: "I've followed [company] since [project/product]…"
Skill match: "My background in [X] aligns with your [team/product]…"
Growth angle: "I want to grow in [area] and your [role/team] offers…"
Closing: "I believe I can contribute [specific value] from day one."
